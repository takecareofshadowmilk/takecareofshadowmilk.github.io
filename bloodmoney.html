About BloodMoney

Bloodmoney! isn’t just another clicker game—it’s a psychological experiment wrapped in minimalist gameplay. Instead of focusing on upgrades, levels, or flashy graphics, the game presents you with one haunting scenario: every click earns you money, but at the expense of a character named Harvey. With each action, Harvey’s condition declines, revealing visible and emotional changes that force you to question how far you’ll go for profit. This stripped-down design turns a simple mechanic into a moral dilemma, blending temptation with discomfort to critique greed, empathy, and human decision-making.

Gameplay Concept

At its core, Bloodmoney! is about choice. Do you chase endless riches while ignoring the consequences, or do you stop short, sparing Harvey further suffering? The outcomes aren’t fixed; instead, your actions guide the story toward multiple endings—from amassing a fortune to discovering the weight of restraint.

How to Play Bloodmoney!

  • Start the Game: Launch directly in your browser or app, and you’ll find Harvey in a stark digital space.

  • Click for Money: Tap or click to generate income, but watch as Harvey reacts through changing expressions, sounds, and physical decline.

  • Track Progress: Your earnings are displayed on screen while Harvey’s state reflects the cost of your actions.

  • Decide Your Path: Continue pressing forward for more wealth, or pause to see what unfolds when compassion outweighs greed.

  • Reach an Ending: Depending on your choices, you’ll unlock different finales that offer fresh perspectives on morality and human behavior.

Why Play?

Bloodmoney! transforms the clicker genre into something far deeper—a tense, thought-provoking reflection on desire, consequence, and self-control. It’s not just about winning; it’s about asking yourself: what is the true price of greed?
